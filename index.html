Submit Your Complaint

If you placed an order on ElectroPi.in and faced issues such as non-delivery, refund delay, no response from support, account access problems, or payment-related concerns, you can submit your experience using the form below.

Several customers have reported concerns relating to ElectroPi.in, including order fulfillment delays, refund delays, difficulty contacting support, and account-related issues. This registry provides a structured way to collect and organize those experiences.

This initiative aims to:

  • Collect customer complaints in one organized place.
  • Preserve supporting evidence such as invoices, receipts, and communication records.
  • Identify repeated patterns in delivery, refund, and support-related issues.
  • Help affected customers prepare better documentation for escalation if required.

Recommended Escalation Steps for Customers

Before or after submitting your complaint here, affected customers may consider the following steps:

  1. Contact ElectroPi.in support and keep written proof of communication.
  2. Request a clear delivery or refund status by email.
  3. If payment was processed through Cashfree Payments, customers may also consider contacting Cashfree and providing transaction details for review.
  4. Contact your bank, card issuer, UPI app, or payment provider regarding transaction disputes where applicable.
  5. File a complaint with the National Consumer Helpline.
  6. Use the e-Daakhil portal for consumer dispute filing if the issue remains unresolved.

Some customers have reported that payments were processed through Cashfree Payments. Customers may wish to retain transaction IDs, payment confirmations, invoices, refund requests, and communication records when raising a payment-related inquiry.
