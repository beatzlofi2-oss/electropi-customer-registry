ElectroPi Customer Complaint Registry

A public evidence-collection initiative for customers reporting order fulfillment, refund, account access, and customer support issues relating to ElectroPi.

Important: This registry collects customer-submitted experiences and supporting evidence. Inclusion in this registry does not by itself prove fraud, criminal conduct, or legal liability. Submissions are treated as customer experiences and allegations unless independently verified.

Purpose of This Registry

Multiple customers have publicly reported concerns regarding ElectroPi order fulfillment, refund processing, account access, and customer support responsiveness.

This registry has been created to:

  • Document unresolved customer complaints.
  • Identify recurring patterns in order fulfillment, refund delays, and customer support.
  • Collect supporting evidence from affected customers.
  • Prepare consolidated evidence for submission to payment processors, consumer authorities, regulators, or legal representatives where appropriate.

Submit Your Experience

If you placed an order with ElectroPi and experienced non-delivery, refund delay, account access issues, or lack of response from customer support, please submit your experience with supporting evidence.

How Submissions Are Used

  • Submissions are reviewed before any public use.
  • Personal information will not be published without consent.
  • Anonymized complaint data may be used for statistics and escalation.
  • False, abusive, duplicate, or unsupported submissions may be removed.

Suggested Escalation Routes

Affected customers may consider contacting the merchant, the payment gateway used for the transaction, their bank or card issuer, the National Consumer Helpline, or the appropriate consumer dispute forum. This website is not legal advice.
